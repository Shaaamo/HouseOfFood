Our History

Like any other original place, we have our own, special history. The idea of the restaurant came to the founders unexpectedly. During a walk through the forest, the founder of our restaurant got stuck hundreds of kilometers from the nearest settlement. Far from civilization and communication, they had to temporarily arrange a simple life for themselves, get and cook their own food.
